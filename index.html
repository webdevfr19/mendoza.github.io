Projects

2010-2012

Mabuhay Residences

The architectural design focuses on open-plan living, sustainable materials, and energy efficiency. Large windows and glass doors are used extensively to maximize natural light and create a seamless connection between indoor and outdoor spaces.

2011-2013

Semion Commercial Building

The design emphasizes a blend of functionality, flexibility, and modern aesthetics. The building features a sleek, contemporary facade with a focus on energy efficiency and occupant comfort.

2013-2015

Mendoza Hillside Residence

The design emphasizes harmony with the environment, maximizing the panoramic views, and incorporating sustainable practices. The residence features clean lines, natural materials, and large windows to create a seamless connection between the indoors and outdoors.

2013-2016

Dela Cruz Rest House

The design focuses on creating a relaxing and inviting atmosphere, combining rustic charm with modern amenities. Large windows and open spaces are key elements, allowing natural light to flood in and offering unobstructed views of the surrounding landscape.
